Sports Recommendation

Sports Available at King's College

Golf

Golf is an outdoor sport where players hit a ball into holes using the fewest number of shots possible.

Tennis

Tennis is a racket sport played against another player or in teams of two.

Basketball

Basketball is a team sport where players try to score by shooting the ball into a hoop.

Swimming

Swimming is a water sport where athletes race using different swimming styles.

Rugby

Rugby is a team sport where players run, pass and kick a ball to score points.
